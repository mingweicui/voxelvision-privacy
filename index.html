VoxelVision Privacy Policy

Spatial Medical Image Viewer for Apple Vision Pro

Overview

VoxelVision is a spatial medical image viewing tool designed for personal exploration, education, and research purposes. It is not intended for clinical diagnosis. This privacy policy explains how VoxelVision handles your data.

In short: VoxelVision does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. All data stays on your device.

Subject Data Protection

Imported DICOM imaging data is processed and stored entirely on the local device and is never uploaded to any server or cloud service. VoxelVision does not collect, transmit, or share any DICOM subject information, including patient name, patient ID, birth date, or other protected health information (PHI).

User Data Protection

VoxelVision does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user information or device identifiers. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising frameworks, or any third-party data collection services.

Runtime Logs

VoxelVision includes built-in runtime logs for troubleshooting purposes. These logs do not contain any DICOM protected health information (PHI) or user privacy data. Log content is stored locally and is only exported when you explicitly choose to do so through the in-app "Logs & Feedback" page. Exported logs are saved to the VoxelVision folder in the Files app for you to review before sharing.

In-App Purchases

VoxelVision offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store infrastructure. The developer does not have access to your payment information.

World Sensing

VoxelVision uses Apple's ARKit world tracking in immersive mode to position and orient 3D medical visualizations in your physical space. World sensing data is processed on-device by the operating system and is not accessed, stored, or transmitted by VoxelVision.

Third-Party Services

VoxelVision does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates entirely offline with no network communication.

Children's Privacy

VoxelVision is a professional medical imaging tool and is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of VoxelVision after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer at voxelvisionsupport@gmail.com.
