VoxelVision Privacy Policy

Spatial Medical Image Viewer for Apple Vision Pro

Overview

VoxelVision is a spatial CT viewing tool designed for personal exploration, education, and research purposes. It is not intended for clinical diagnosis. All imaging data is processed and displayed locally in read-only mode for spatial visualization.

In short: VoxelVision does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. All data stays on your device.

Compliance Statement

The design of this software follows the relevant principles of the following standards and regulations:

  • DICOM Medical Imaging Communication Standard
  • HIPAA Privacy Rule (Protected Health Information management principles)
  • ISO 13485 Medical Device Quality Management System (design reference)
  • ISO/IEC 27001 Information Security Management System (design reference)

Subject Data Protection

Imported DICOM imaging data is processed and stored entirely on the local device and is never uploaded to any server or cloud service. This software does not collect, transmit, or share any DICOM subject information, including name, subject ID, date of birth, or other protected health information (PHI).

User Data Protection

This software does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user information or device identifiers. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising frameworks, or any third-party data collection services.

Runtime Logs

VoxelVision includes built-in runtime logs for troubleshooting purposes. These logs do not contain any DICOM protected health information (PHI) or user privacy data. You can view and export log content on the in-app "Logs & Feedback" page. Exported logs are saved to the VoxelVision folder in the Files app for you to review before sharing.

In-App Purchases

VoxelVision offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store infrastructure. The developer does not have access to your payment information.

World Sensing

VoxelVision uses Apple's ARKit world tracking in immersive mode to position and orient 3D medical visualizations in your physical space. World sensing data is processed on-device by the operating system and is not accessed, stored, or transmitted by VoxelVision.

Third-Party Services

VoxelVision does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates entirely offline with no network communication.

Sample Data

VoxelVision includes a sample CT dataset from the LIDC-IDRI collection, hosted by The Cancer Imaging Archive (TCIA), licensed under CC BY 3.0. This dataset is publicly available de-identified data and contains no personally identifiable information.

Data Collection

VoxelVision does not collect any data from its users. The app is suitable for users of all ages. No information is gathered, stored, or shared with any party.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of VoxelVision after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer at voxelvisionsupport@gmail.com.
